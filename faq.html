Frequently Asked Questions 

Where did the name come from?
There are always things in your life that don't get enough attention. They are saying "give me some love"!
I tapped the heart by mistake. How do I undo it?
Tap the heart again to undo.
Is there a way I can see the statistics and history for an activity?
Keeping a running history for each activity would make the app more complicated, and we decided that keeping things simple was more important.
I gave an activity some love a while ago, but I forgot to tap the heart. What do I do?
Press and hold the heart. Then you can choose how long ago you gave an activity some love.
