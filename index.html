Give Me Love

Make a plan.
And stick with it.


Make a plan

You have goals you want to accomplish and routines you want to stick with, but somehow they slip through the cracks. What you need is a good plan, one that you can stick with week after week. Give Me Love can help.

Need more exercise? Put "Go for a run" in your plan. Want to learn an instrument? Add "Practice piano"!

Give it some love

Take that run you have been meaning to do for weeks. Tap the heart and watch it pulse with satisfaction. You are on your way!

It does not matter if you spend 2 minutes or 2 hours on an activity. The trick is to keep at it. Give Me Love helps you stay on track. 

See what's next

Wondering what do next? The activities that need love rise to the top. You can see when you last did an activity and when you should do it again.

Spend 5 minutes every day cleaning the garage because its in the plan. After a few weeks, you finally have room to store your old golf clubs! 

Stay on track

Let's face it, we all forget to do things, even when they are important. Give Me Love will notify you when your plan needs some attention. These daily reminders motivate you to take action and keep your plan moving forward.

You might be in the middle of something, but every day when "Practice piano" pops up, you take a break and play for 15 minutes. Two months later you wow your family with Fur Elise!

Be happy

Week in and week out you stick with your plan and give it the love it deserves. The things that are important to you are getting done and you feel great about it.

Life is full of surprises, and it is not always easy to follow through on all the commitments you make to yourself, but by spending a little time every day on the things that matter, you will be happier and more motivated.

Time to get started!

There are so many things you want to do. Don't put them off any longer. Download Give Me Love and make a plan you can stick with. You will be amazed at what you can get done.

Make a plan today.
